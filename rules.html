Bubble Game Rules

Objective

Pop the bubbles with the right number to score points!

Game Setup

  • The timer starts at 60 seconds.
  • There are 147 bubbles with numbers from 0 to 9.
  • The target number is shown at the top.

Scoring

  • Pop a bubble with the target number to get 10 points.
  • Your score is shown at the top.

Gameplay

  • Click on the bubbles to find the target number.
  • A new target number appears after each correct click.

Timer

  • The timer counts down from 60 seconds.
  • The remaining time is shown at the top.

Game Over

  • The game ends when the timer reaches zero.
  • Your final score is shown.

Extra Rules

  • Clicking wrong bubbles does nothing.
  • Keep playing until the timer runs out.
